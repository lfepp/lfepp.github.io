ScheduleDuty

ScheduleDuty is a PyPi package that allows you to create schedules in PagerDuty by importing a CSV. I have also built a front-end form for the package that can be easily deployed to Heroku and entered into a PagerDuty account as an add-on. The front-end is available here.
