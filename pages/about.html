About

My Story

My name is Luke and I am a self-taught web developer, solutions consultant, and anthropologist based out of Washington, DC. I am passionate about implementing simple yet effective solutions to complex problems and my diverse skill set grants me the unique perspective necessary to excel at this task. For instance, my experience consulting enterprise clients on the implementation of software solutions as well as my studies of the human condition provide me with the empathy and understanding necessary to design excellent user stories prior to the start of development on a project. I leverage these skills at every step of the development life cycle to design the best possible solution for the client, implement the solution seamlessly, and iterate on the solution where necessary to accomplish the client's goals.
